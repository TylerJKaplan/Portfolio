THERE'S ONE WAY TO FIGHT BACK: GET OUTSIDE. LOG OFF.

Print media is back baby! Every time we get out and log off, we weaken these tech oligarchs ability to rule our lives. Meta Palentire Oracle Fuck 'em

This is a magazine. Well it is a website which will eventually be turned into a magazine and it exists to inspire activity. We're building a community of contributors and readers who feed off each other's energy. Share your stories, your art, your weird niche interests. We will then turn those items into physical print media. They can't take that away from us. Until your apartment burns down from increasing wild fires. Then your copy is lost forever.

Whatever your passion is, share it in fun and unique ways.

Don't have passions? Neither do I. I like graphic design, getting drunk, and yelling at my TV when some 19 year old kid drops a football. I made this because I want to be inspired. I don't have much to share yet, but I hope you do or maybe you'll be inspired to pick up something new.

WHAT WE WANT:

Your stories. Your art. Your niche interests.

  • Poetry
  • Photography
  • Food blogging
  • Stories and fucks ups
  • Woodworking projects (even the ugly ones)
  • That weird hobby you haven't told anyone about
  • Anything you made or experinced that got you away from the screen

ALL SUBMISSIONS ARE ANONYMOUS. We don't want you forever linked to a shitty essay. And we definitly don't want you to use this to flex how sick you are.

Maybe you don't have anything to share yet. That's cool—hopefully something here inspires you to pick up something new. We're all figuring it out.

THE RULES (SORT OF):

1. No identifying info. We'll keep you anonymous. If you want a cool pen name or submit pictures with faces you want blurred let us know

2. Keep it real. Lying is no fun.

3. Mistakes and failures are more interesting than perfection.

4. If it got you outside or making something, it counts.

5. Be chill.
